Our Story

Come in. Breathe. Chill.

  • Walker Evans, Owner

    On my last day of exams I didn’t feel like going to my test and realized that the hookah bar I wanted to open when I graduated would cost less to start up now, than to finish school. So I dropped out, went to Arlington, and recruited my managers.

  • Justin Reynolds, Manager

    Walker came to me one night when I was working and asked me if I would like to open a hookah bar with him. I thought about it for about a day, then I said, “fuck it lets do it”. I quit school quit my job and moved to Lubbock, and here I am.

  • Dustin Duong, Manager

    While I was chilling on my off day at the hookah lounge that both Justin and I worked at, Walker approached me with an idea of opening a hookah lounge, which at first I thought we were just joking about it again. But turned out that he was actually serious and blatantly told me that he quit school that day and I said, “Well then, fuck it, I’m down!”
